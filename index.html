01 — About

A little about my process

I'm a final-year digital media student who fell in love with the idea that a still object — a chair, a pair of headphones, a coiled cable — can be built up from nothing but vertices and light. My work sits at the intersection of technical modeling and expressive animation.

Outside of coursework, I script my own Maya and MEL tools to speed up repetitive tasks, and I edit short films in Premiere Pro just to keep my eye sharp for pacing and color.
